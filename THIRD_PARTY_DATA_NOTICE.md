# Third-party X data notice

This repository distributes only the Post IDs needed to identify the public X
posts evaluated in the accompanying research. It does not distribute hydrated
post text, public account data, profile descriptions, timestamps, engagement
snapshots, or profile metrics.

Users who choose to retrieve current Post data must do so directly from X,
using their own approved credentials and in accordance with the current X
Terms, Developer Agreement, Developer Policy, rate limits, and deletion or
withholding requirements. Hydrated content must remain outside version control.

Posts may have been deleted, edited, protected, withheld, or otherwise become
unavailable since collection. Consequently, a newly hydrated dataset may not
reproduce the historical paper snapshot exactly.

The authors do not grant a license to third-party X content and do not imply
endorsement by X or by the authors of the underlying posts.
