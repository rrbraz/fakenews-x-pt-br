## Explicit exclusions

CC BY 4.0 does not apply to Post IDs as third-party platform identifiers, and
does not apply to any X post text, URL, timestamp, account
name, username, profile description, engagement count, profile count, media,
or other hydrated X content.

The free-text LLM rationales and row-level BERT embeddings used internally in
the paper are not included in this conservative public distribution because
they may preserve or encode third-party post content. The published structured
labels remain researcher-created annotations.
